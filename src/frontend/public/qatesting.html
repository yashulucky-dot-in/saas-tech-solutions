OVERVIEW

Delivering Quality at Every Stage

Quality is not a phase — it is a discipline. Our QA practice is embedded throughout the entire software development lifecycle, catching defects early and ensuring your product meets the highest standards before it reaches your users. From agile sprints to enterprise release cycles, we tailor our testing approach to fit your delivery model and technology stack.

WHAT WE DELIVER

Our QA Capabilities

End-to-end quality assurance services covering every layer of your application stack

Manual Testing

Functional, regression, usability, and exploratory testing by expert QA engineers who understand your business logic and user journeys.

Automated Testing

Selenium, Cypress, and Playwright test automation frameworks — reducing regression cycles and accelerating release velocity at scale.

Performance Testing

Load, stress, and scalability testing with JMeter to identify bottlenecks and capacity limits before they impact your production users.

API Testing

RESTful API validation and contract testing using Postman — covering endpoint correctness, edge cases, error handling, and data integrity.

Security Testing

OWASP vulnerability testing and penetration testing to harden your applications against real-world threats and compliance requirements.

Mobile Testing

iOS and Android app testing across devices and simulators with Appium and BrowserStack — functional, UI, and device-specific scenario coverage.

HOW WE WORK

Our QA Process

A structured, repeatable five-step approach that delivers quality at every stage of the software lifecycle

STEP 01

Plan

Test strategy definition, scope analysis, risk assessment, and comprehensive test plan creation aligned with project milestones.

STEP 02

Design

Test case design, test data preparation, and automation framework setup — building a reusable, maintainable testing architecture.

STEP 03

Execute

Manual and automated test execution cycles with real-time defect tracking, reproduction steps, and severity classification in JIRA.

STEP 04

Report

Detailed test summary reports with pass/fail metrics, code coverage, defect density, and full traceability back to requirements.

STEP 05

Improve

Lessons learned, regression suite optimisation, and ongoing quality improvements — building sustainable quality practices into your team.
